Comparison with Data Rich

MSE will be used to evaluate alternative data-poor MPs for highly migratory stocks. We do this by comparing candidate procedures to an idealised data-rich one using the benefit-cost ratio (BCR) as an indicator. The approach allows the benefits of reducing uncertainty and the associated risks to the resources and fishing communities to be evaluated. We then discuss the costs and benefits of alternative choices, i.e. of investing in science, data collection or management, consistent with the PA and the scientific advice framework of the tRFMOs.
