Data Poor

There are two areas of work, related to data poor stocks and methods, i.e. using MSE to develop management advice frameworks for i) the data poor tuna stocks (e.g. in the Mediterranean and South Atlantic) based on work done for data rich stocks (e.g. North Atlantic albacore); and ii) by caught species such as sharks.
