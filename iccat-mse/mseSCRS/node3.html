North Atlantic Albacore

The albacore Species Group conducted a preliminary MSE in 2013 kell2013msealbn and used this to propose a interim LRP for use as part of a HCR. The HCR and the LRP was based upon a biomass dynamic stock assessment kell2013mpalbn and the OM was conditioned on a Multifan-CL stock assessment kell2013msealbn. This LRP was then used to produce a k2sm and provide advice to the Commission kell2013fwd. However, the HCR still needs to be fully evaluated.

The MSE framework is implemented in R, R using FLR kell_flr:_2007. The biomass dynamic model includes a variety of methods to estimate uncertainty, i.e. maximum likelihood, bootstrap and Bayesian kell2013uncert for use as part of a MP.
