MSE Electronic Working Group

At the Third Joint Tuna RFMOs meeting (Kobe III) it was recognised that Management Strategy Evaluation (MSE) needs to be widely implemented in the tRFMOs in order to implement a Precautionary Approach for tuna fisheries management. It was therefotre recommended that a Joint MSE Technical Working Group be created to work electronically, in the first instance.

The work of the five tRFMO Scientific Committees can be found via their websites

 
  CCSBT - Commission for the Conservation of Southern Bluefin Tuna
  IATTC - Inter-American Tropical Tuna Commission
  ICCAT - International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas
  IOTC - Indian Ocean Tuna Commission
  WCPFC - Western and Central Pacific Fisheries Commission
   

 

Activities


The activities of the WG are intended to aid in the implementation of MSE and the development of tools and methodologies by promoting collaboration, i.e. by sharing code and data, working on collaborative papers and keeping each other updated on the various activities being conducted by the tRFMOs. Activities of fall under several catagories

  • Calender A google calender that members can add evants and meeting dates to
  • Software Links to software repositories
  • Cloud and Parallel Computing Links to tools and sites for high power compuitng
  • Bibliography References to peer review and grey literature in Zotero
  • Collaborative Papers
  • Glossary
To get involved join the tRFMO-MSE google email group.
or contact Laurence Kell.

Summary of ongoing work

CCSBT have developed a Management Procedure (MP) for southern bluefin using MSE; IOTC are using MSE to evaluate Limit Refence Points (LRPs) for albacore and skipjack; IATTC do not intend to use MSE; ICCAT are using MSE to evaluate LPRs as part of a Harvest Control Rule (HCR) fortemperate tunas and swordfish. A new management framework will also be developed for bluefin using MSE; and WCPFC are using simulation to without feedback to evaluate LRPs

  • CCSBT - Southern Bluefin Management Procedure
  • IATTC - No Activity
  • ICCAT - Limit Reference Points and Bluefin
  • IOTC - Albacore and Skipjack
  • WCPFC - Simulation of HCRs without feedback
