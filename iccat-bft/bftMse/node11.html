Outline: Detailed Work Plan for Conducting the Management Strategy Evaluation

This section outlines, in tabular form, a schedule for the work required to conduct the 2014 and 2015 assessments and then to evaluate a management procedure using an operating model for Atlantic bluefin. A detailed work plan, based on this schedule, will be developed for presentation at the SCRS plenary. Following endorsement by the SCRS, a budget will be proposed for presentation to the Commission. To implement the operating model, it is essential that contracts for providing external support for a number of years are awarded. It is also essential to establish a core steering group to oversee the work.
