tRFMO-MSE

At the Third Joint Tuna RFMOs meeting (i.e. Kobe III) it was recognised that Management Strategy Evaluation needs to be widely implemented in the tRFMOs in order to implement a Precautionary Approach for tuna fisheries management. It was therefore recommended that a Joint MSE Technical Working Group be created to work electronicall.
